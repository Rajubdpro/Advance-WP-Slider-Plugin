== Description ==

Advance WP Slider is a powerful and versatile plugin that allows you to create stunning sliders and carousels on your WordPress website. Showcase your content, products, or images with style using our user-friendly interface and robust customization options.

== Features ==

- **Responsive Design:** Sliders automatically adjust to various screen sizes for optimal viewing on any device.
- **Multiple Transition Effects:** Choose from a range of captivating transition effects to enhance the visual appeal of your sliders.
- **Customizable Styles:** Tailor the appearance of your sliders by customizing colors, fonts, and more.
- **Image Captions:** Add captions and descriptions to your slider images to provide context and engage your audience.
- **Shortcode Integration:** Easily embed sliders into posts, pages, or widgets using shortcodes.
- **Touch and Swipe Support:** Sliders are touch-friendly, ensuring a seamless user experience on mobile devices.
- **SEO Friendly:** Sliders are designed to be search engine friendly, helping to improve your site's SEO.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Create and manage sliders effortlessly with our intuitive admin panel.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `advance-wp-slider` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the plugin settings by navigating to the 'Advance WP Slider' section in the WordPress admin panel.

== Usage ==

1. Create a new slider by navigating to the 'Advance WP Slider' menu in your admin dashboard.
2. Add slides to your slider, including images, captions, and links.
3. Customize the slider settings to match your website's design and preferences.
4. Use the provided shortcode to insert the slider into any post, page, or widget.

Shortcode Example: [AWP-SLIDER]
